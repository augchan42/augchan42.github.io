Service Offerings

AI-Assisted Development Training & Mentoring

Ready to start immediately • Most accessible offering

Transform how your team codes with comprehensive training on AI-assisted development workflows. I've been using Cursor since its first release and have witnessed the evolution from primitive chat with copy-paste to automatic file updates, and now agents that loop and update many files at once.

Your team will learn to think strategically about AI-assisted tools (Cursor Agent Mode, Claude Code, MCP) - understanding what's happening under the hood, recognizing the challenges, and leveraging these tools effectively in their daily workflow.

Full-day intensive or two consecutive half-days - your choice based on team preference.

  • Full-day intensive: Morning setup + demo, afternoon guided project work. One commitment, done in a day.
  • Two consecutive half-days: Day 1 setup + demo, Day 2 guided project work. Less intensive per day, easier to absorb.
  • Benefits: Both avoid momentum loss of spaced sessions and work well for team scheduling
  • Audience: Development teams, engineering managers, and individual developers
  • No preliminary consultation required - we can jump straight into scheduling

The consecutive half-days option is often ideal because people can sleep on concepts overnight, it's less mentally exhausting than 8 hours straight, maintains momentum and context, and the environment stays set up between sessions.
