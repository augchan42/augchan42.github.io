Working Together

Why Work With Me?

For over two decades, I've been in the business of turning ambitious ideas into tangible, scalable software. I don't just write code; I engineer solutions. My career has been about tackling the hard problems head-on. For example, during my time at Informatica, I re-architected critical systems to achieve a 200% performance boost by implementing linear scaling and dynamic workload distribution. More recently, I've been pioneering in the Web3 and AI space, building 8-Bit Oracle, an innovative project using digital divination that won at the Consensus HK Hackathon.

I also love partnering with driven founders. I've helped ventures like People App and Travelbox, bringing not just deep engineering expertise but also a founder's mindset to every challenge.

So, whether you need an MVP built, your current systems scaled, or a seasoned technical leader to guide your strategy, I offer a blend of hard-won experience, a knack for cutting-edge tech, and the relentless drive of a startup builder. Here's how we can work together:
